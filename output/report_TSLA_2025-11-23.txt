## 1. Company Overview

Tesla, Inc. (TSLA) is a leading innovator in the electric vehicle (EV) and clean energy sectors. Headquartered in Austin, Texas, Tesla designs, manufactures, and sells high-performance EVs, including the Model 3, Model Y, Model S, Model X, Cybertruck, and Semi. Beyond automotive, the company offers energy generation and storage solutions such as solar panels, Solar Roof, and Powerwall/Powerpack batteries. Tesla's mission is to accelerate the world's transition to sustainable energy.
